# Chladni Resonance Patterns - Story Through Physics

## The Storytelling Approach

Like a real Chladni plate vibrating at different frequencies, each section creates a distinctive resonance pattern. The particles flow smoothly from one pattern to another, creating a subtle visual narrative that supports the content.

**Key Principle:** The patterns should be beautiful, subtle, and meaningful - not decorative. Each pattern reinforces the emotional and conceptual beat of that section.

## Transition Choreography

### The Flow Between Sections

Transitions should feel like watching a real Chladni plate as someone adjusts the frequency. The particles don't teleport - they flow from one pattern to another.

**Transition Duration:** 2 seconds (120 frames at 60fps)
**Easing:** Cubic ease-in-out (matches natural resonance changes)

### Critical Transitions

#### HERO → PROBLEM
**What happens:** Structure dissolves into chaos
**Visual:** Particles drift away from clear lines into scattered randomness
**Speed:** Medium (gradual decay of order)
**Emotional impact:** "Oh no, we're losing structure"

#### PROBLEM → ANSWER
**What happens:** Chaos snaps into three clear pillars
**Visual:** Particles accelerate toward three vertical structures, radiating outward
**Speed:** Fast (decisive transformation)
**Emotional impact:** "Aha! This is the solution!"
**Special:** This is THE KEY TRANSITION - should feel dramatic and liberating

#### ANSWER → PLATFORM
**What happens:** Three pillars expand into four quadrants
**Visual:** Pattern grows more complex, adds structure
**Speed:** Medium (organic growth)
**Emotional impact:** "It's expanding into something complete"

#### BUNDLE → ECOSYSTEM
**What happens:** Simple unity becomes dense network
**Visual:** Pattern densifies, connections multiply
**Speed:** Medium-slow (organic evolution)
**Emotional impact:** "Look at all the possibilities"

## Technical Parameters

### Force Strength
- **High (1.0-1.2):** Particles snap into place quickly (Answer, CTA)
- **Medium (0.8-0.9):** Balanced, smooth flow (Hero, Platform, Bundle)
- **Low (0.1-0.3):** Weak structure, scattered (Problem)

### Damping
- **High (0.94+):** Very smooth, graceful (Bundle, Answer)
- **Medium (0.92):** Normal smooth flow (Hero, Platform)
- **Low (0.85):** Less smooth, more erratic (Problem)

### Chaos
- **0:** Ordered patterns (all sections except Problem)
- **1.5:** High randomness (Problem only)

---

## Visual Subtlety Guidelines

1. **Opacity:** Never go above 0.6 - these patterns should support, not dominate
2. **Color:** Adapt to section background (darker for light sections, lighter for dark sections)
3. **Particle Size:** 2-3px - visible but not distracting
4. **Glow:** Only on nodal lines, subtle (0.4 intensity max)
5. **Animation:** Particles should always be in motion, never completely still
